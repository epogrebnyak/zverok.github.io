    rb_eStopIteration

    class Ractor::ClosedError

    Raised when an attempt is made to take something from the Ractor's outgoing port, but it is closed with Ractor#close_outgoing, or to send something to Ractor's incoming port, and it is closed with Ractor#close_incoming, or an attempt to send/take something with ractor which was already terminated.

    r = Ractor.new { sleep(500) }
r.close_outgoing
r.take # Ractor::ClosedError
